Piano Lessons

We offer piano lessons for all ages, beginning at age three. Whether you are a beginner or have been playing the piano for a while. We have a program to suit your needs. Learn new techniques, theory, harmony, and tone. Learning how to play the piano helps develop hand-eye coordination and self-confidence.

Guitar Lessons

We teach acoustic guitar, electic guitar, bass guitar, ukulele. We have guitar lessons for beginners, intermediate players, and advanced players. Improve your skills with weekly lessons. Learn new tips and techniques from experienced Instructors. We also have a rock band program that you can join!

Violin Lessons

We offer violin lessons for all ages, beginner at six. Our violin lessons include a variety of music styles, from fiddles to classical. Our instructors will help fine-tune techniques and accomplish your musical goals. We work with you to create a customized program, tailored to your needs.
